Overview

A controlled environment for simulation-led decisions.

Engineering teams often lose time translating technical intent into repeatable computational studies. galerkin focuses on shortening that path while preserving review, traceability, and expert control.

The platform is designed for exploratory research, early engineering evaluation, and structured technical reporting. It is not positioned as a replacement for domain expertise, certification, or independent validation.

Process

How access begins.

Request access

The organisation submits a short description of the engineering question, domain, expected deliverables, and data sensitivity.

Scope review

galerkin reviews project fit, technical boundaries, confidentiality needs, and validation expectations.

Access agreement

Access is provided after scope, delivery format, confidentiality, and usage limitations are agreed in writing.

Controlled delivery

The customer receives structured project outputs and technical reporting prepared for expert review and independent validation.

Validation

Designed for expert review.

galerkin supports preparation, organisation, execution management, and reporting of computational studies. Scientific judgment remains with the customer’s qualified researchers and engineers.

Customers are responsible for confirming assumptions, reviewing technical inputs, validating results, and deciding whether an output is suitable for any downstream use.

Terms

Use limitations.

The platform may use automated computational methods, statistical models, and software-assisted workflow generation to support study preparation, execution management, and reporting.

Outputs are provided for research, exploratory engineering, and expert review. They are not certified engineering advice and must not be used as the sole basis for safety-critical, regulatory, production, medical, aerospace-certification, or infrastructure decisions.

Customer technical data is handled as confidential project material and is not reused for third-party product development without written permission.
