Purpose

A controlled environment for simulation-led decisions.

Engineering teams often lose time translating technical intent into repeatable computational studies. galerkin focuses on shortening that path while preserving review, traceability, and expert control.

The platform is designed for exploratory research, early engineering evaluation, and structured technical reporting. It is not positioned as a replacement for domain expertise, certification, or independent validation.
